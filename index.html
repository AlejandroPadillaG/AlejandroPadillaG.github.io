About Me

I’m Francisco Alejandro Padilla Gómez, a final‑year PhD candidate at the Center for Computational Mathematics, Example University. My research blends high‑performance numerical analysis, stochastic modelling, and GPU acceleration—skills directly relevant to national laboratories and quantitative finance groups seeking rigorous solutions to large‑scale, uncertainty‑aware problems.

Research Interests

  • Entropy‑stable high‑order methods for nonlinear PDEs
  • Stochastic spectral methods & uncertainty quantification
  • GPU‑accelerated solvers and mixed‑precision algorithms
  • Risk‑sensitive optimal control & portfolio PDEs

Selected Publications
